Schedule 5 
 
Amounts for Spouse or Common-Law Partner and Dependants 
 
Complete this schedule and attach it to your return to claim an amount on 
line 303, 304, 305, or 307 of your Schedule 1. For more information, see the 
corresponding line number in the Income Tax and Benefit Guide.
