T1 General - 2010 
 
Schedule BC(S11) 
 
Provincial Tuition and Education Amounts 
 
Only the student must complete this schedule. Use it to: 
 
- calculate your British Columbia tuition and education amounts to claim on 
line 5856 of your Form BC428; 
 
- determine the provincial amount available to transfer to another designated 
individual; and 
 
- determine the unused provincial amount, if any, available for you to carry 
forward to a future year. 
 
Only the student attaches a copy of this schedule to his or her return.
