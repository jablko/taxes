Schedule 11 
 
Tuition, Education, and Textbook Amounts 
 
For more information, see line 323 in the guide. 
 
Only the student must complete this schedule and attach it to his or her 
return. Use it to: 
 
- calculate your federal tuition, education, and textbook amounts; 
 
- determine the federal amount available to transfer to a designated 
individual; and 
 
- determine the unused federal amount, if any, available for you to carry 
forward to a future year.
